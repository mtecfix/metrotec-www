**Opening:**
"Hi [First Name], this is [Your Name]. I'm calling about automated product ad generation for [Store Name]. Do you have 2 minutes?"

"Great! Quick question: How much time do you spend creating ads for new arrivals? We've built an AI that generates professional ads in 60 seconds. [Competitor] increased sales 41%. Can I show you a quick demo this week?"

**"We post on Instagram ourselves"**
→ "Perfect! This makes that easier. Instead of spending 2 hours per post, spend 10 minutes. Want to see how [Competitor] does it?"

→ "I get it. That's why we offer free trial. You'll see more sales before spending a dollar. Fair?"

→ "Sure, but info is boring. Can I send a 2-minute video showing your products turned into ads? You'll see exactly what you'd get."

## Personalization Checklist

Before sending:
- [ ] Store name (3+ times)
- [ ] Specific product/collection (check Instagram)
- [ ] Recent sale or event
- [ ] Seasonal relevance (spring, holiday, etc.)
- [ ] Competitor name (similar store)
- [ ] Their posting frequency (research Instagram)
- [ ] Recent Instagram post (reference it)

---

## Best Times to Send

**Retailers check email:**
- Tuesday-Thursday: 9am-10am (before store opens)
- Tuesday-Thursday: 2pm-3pm (slow afternoon)

**Avoid:**
- Friday-Sunday (busy with customers)
- Monday (restocking, planning)
- 11am-1pm (lunch rush)
