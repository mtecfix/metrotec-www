## Phone Script

**Opening:**
"Hi [First Name], this is [Your Name]. I'm calling about filling classes at [Business Name]. Do you have 2 minutes?"

**If YES:**
"Great! Quick question: How full are your weekday morning classes? We've built an AI that generates class-specific ads and fills empty spots. [Competitor] increased attendance 47%. Can I show you a quick demo?"

**If NO:**
"No problem. Can I text you a 2-minute video? What's your cell?"

**Objection Handling:**

**"We post on Instagram ourselves"**
→ "Perfect! This makes that easier. Instead of spending 2 hours per day, spend 10 minutes per week. Want to see how?"

**"We don't have budget"**
→ "I get it. But every empty class spot is lost revenue. This pays for itself if it fills just 3 extra spots per week. Want to see the math?"

**"We're too busy with clients"**
→ "That's exactly why you need this! It works while you're training clients. Takes 10 minutes to set up. Can I show you?"

**"Send me information"**
→ "Sure, but can I send a 2-minute video showing ads for your actual classes? You'll see exactly what you'd get."

## Personalization Checklist

Before sending:
- [ ] Business name (3+ times)
- [ ] Specific class type (yoga, spin, HIIT, etc.)
- [ ] Recent Instagram post (reference it)
- [ ] Competitor name (similar studio)
- [ ] Their class schedule (check website)
- [ ] Current promotion (if any)
- [ ] Nearby landmark (for targeting context)

---

## Best Times to Send

**Fitness businesses check email:**
- Tuesday-Thursday: 8am-9am (before classes start)
- Tuesday-Thursday: 1pm-2pm (between classes)

**Avoid:**
- Monday (planning week)
- Friday-Sunday (busy with classes)
- 5am-7am (teaching morning classes)
- 5pm-7pm (teaching evening classes)

---

## Success Metrics

- Open rate: Target 40%+
- Reply rate: Target 11%+
- Demo booked: Target 6%+
- Close rate: Target 32%+

**If below targets:**
- Add more class-specific personalization
- Reference transformation posts
- Test different seasonal hooks
- Send Tuesday 8am vs. Thursday 1pm
