## Phone Script (If You Get Them on the Line)

**Opening:**
"Hi [First Name], this is [Your Name]. I'm calling about automated ad generation for [Hotel Name]. Do you have 2 minutes?"

**If YES:**
"Great! I'll be quick. We help Detroit hotels create professional ads in under 60 seconds using AI. Most hotels spend 10-15 hours/week on ad creation. We cut that to 30 minutes. Can I show you a quick demo this week?"

**If NO:**
"No problem. Can I email you a 2-minute video showing how it works? What's the best email?"

**Objection Handling:**

**"We already have a marketing agency"**
→ "Perfect! This actually makes your agency more efficient. They can focus on strategy while the AI handles execution. Want to see how [Competitor] uses both?"

**"We don't have budget"**
→ "Totally understand. That's why we offer a free 30-day trial. You'll see ROI before spending a dollar. Fair?"

**"Send me information"**
→ "Absolutely. But info is boring. Can I send you a 2-minute video showing ads generated specifically for [Hotel Name]? You'll see exactly what you'd get."

**"Not interested"**
→ "No worries. Can I ask – is it because you're happy with your current ad creation process, or is it just not a priority right now?"

## Personalization Checklist

Before sending, customize:
- [ ] Hotel name (3+ times)
- [ ] Specific event/promotion they're running
- [ ] Competitor name (if mentioning)
- [ ] Their occupancy challenge (research on reviews)
- [ ] Recent social media post (reference it)
- [ ] Upcoming local event (Tigers game, concert, etc.)

---

## Success Metrics to Track

- Open rate: Target 40%+
- Reply rate: Target 10%+
- Demo booked: Target 5%+
- Close rate: Target 30%+

**If below targets:**
- Test different subject lines
- Shorten email body
- Add more specific personalization
- Try different send times (Tuesday 10am works best)
