→ "Sure, but info is boring. Can I send a 2-minute video showing your restaurant's menu turned into ads? You'll see exactly what you'd get."

## Personalization Checklist

Before sending:
- [ ] Restaurant name (3+ times)
- [ ] Specific dish/menu item (check Instagram)
- [ ] Recent event they hosted
- [ ] Nearby venue (Comerica Park, Ford Field, etc.)
- [ ] Competitor name (similar restaurant)
- [ ] Their slow nights (research on OpenTable)
- [ ] Recent Instagram post (reference it)

---

## Best Times to Send

**Restaurants check email:**
- Tuesday-Thursday: 10am-11am (after morning prep)
- Tuesday-Thursday: 2pm-4pm (between lunch and dinner)

**Avoid:**
- Friday-Sunday (too busy)
- Monday (recovering from weekend)
- 5pm-10pm (dinner service)

---

## Success Metrics

- Open rate: Target 45%+
- Reply rate: Target 12%+
- Demo booked: Target 6%+
- Close rate: Target 35%+

**If below targets:**
- Add more food/menu personalization
- Reference specific Instagram posts
- Shorten email (restaurants are busy)
- Test sending Tuesday 10am vs. Thursday 2pm
