Privacy Policy

How MetroTec collects, uses, and protects your personal information

Last Updated: March 20, 2026  ·  Effective: March 20, 2026  ·  Questions? privacy@mrtechfixes.com

1. Introduction

Comprehensive IT Services for Business ("MetroTec," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our IT services. Please read this policy carefully.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, business name
  • Business Information: Industry, company size, IT needs and challenges
  • Service Requests: Details submitted via contact forms or quote requests
  • Communications: Messages, emails, and support tickets you send us

    • Information Collected Automatically

    • Technical Data: IP address, browser type and version, device type and OS
    • Usage Data: Pages visited, time on site, referring URLs, click paths
    • Cookies: Session and persistent cookies (see Section 7)

    3. How We Use Your Information

    We use collected information to:

    • Provide, operate, and improve our IT services
    • Respond to inquiries, quotes, and support requests
    • Send service updates, invoices, and account notifications
    • Send marketing communications (with your consent; opt-out anytime)
    • Analyze website usage to improve user experience
    • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements
    • Detect and prevent fraud or security incidents

    4. Information Sharing

    We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

    • Service Providers: Trusted vendors who assist our operations (e.g., hosting, analytics, billing) under strict confidentiality agreements
    • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government authority
    • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with advance notice)
    • Your Consent: Any other sharing with your explicit permission

    5. Data Security

    We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

    • TLS/SSL encryption for all data in transit
    • Encrypted storage for sensitive data at rest
    • Role-based access controls limiting who can access your data
    • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
    • Employee security training and confidentiality agreements

    No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we use commercially reasonable measures, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

    6. Data Breach Notification

    In the event of a confirmed data breach affecting your personal information, MetroTec will:

    • Notify affected individuals within 72 hours of discovery via email
    • Provide a written incident summary within 5 business days
    • Take immediate steps to contain and remediate the breach
    • Notify relevant authorities as required under Michigan's Identity Theft Protection Act (MCL 445.63) and applicable federal law

    7. Cookies and Tracking

    We use cookies and similar technologies for:

    • Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality (cannot be disabled)
    • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics to understand site usage (can be opted out)
    • Preference Cookies: Remembering your settings and preferences

    You can control or disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality. To opt out of Google Analytics, visit tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

    8. Your Rights

    Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

    • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
    • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
    • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information ("right to be forgotten")
    • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
    • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
    • Restriction: Request we limit processing of your data in certain circumstances

    To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@mrtechfixes.com. We will respond within 30 days.

    9. Data Retention

    We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations:

    • Active client records: Duration of the service relationship plus 7 years
    • Prospect/inquiry data: 2 years from last contact
    • Website analytics: 26 months (Google Analytics default)
    • Legal/compliance records: As required by applicable law

    10. Third-Party Links

    Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

    11. Children's Privacy

    Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, contact us immediately at privacy@mrtechfixes.com.

    12. Changes to This Policy

    Material changes — defined as changes affecting how we collect, use, or share your data — will be communicated to active clients with at least 30 days written notice via email. Non-material updates (e.g., formatting, clarifications) will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of our services after the notice period constitutes acceptance.
